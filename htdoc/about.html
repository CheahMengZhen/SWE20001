About GOTO Grocery


Goto Grocery is a member-based grocery store based in Hawthorn. We offer quality grocery items for our members. Sign up with us to get all latest and best deals there are. Our roots go way back to the early 2000s, in a quiet neighbourhood grocery store in Hawthorn, Victoria . Within 10 years since our inception, Our store quickly became the go-to place for fresh produce, poultry, dairy products as well as a large array of items necessary for daily use. An additional 2 branches were opened in Tasmania (2003), Queensland (2018). All this while, we have recorded our member's records through a simple paper-based method. This year we have decided to completely transform our current method to digital online members records system. This approach will most definately be more environmental friendly and lower our cost of purchasing paper.
