About PCplanet Online Inc


PC Home Online Inc is a Taiwan-based company principally engaged in the provision of electronic commerce, entrance Websites and other related services. The Company operates in electronic commerce business, which includes online shopping, 24-hour shopping and global shopping business, dealing with computer, communication and consumer electronic (3C) products, foods, apparels and books, and others, as well as entrance Website business, which includes the operation of PCplanet online Website. The Company mainly operates its businesses in domestic market.


Milestones

1996

PCplanet Online website launched. Commenced portal operations.

1998

PCplanet Online incorporated as PCplanet Online Inc.

2000

E-commerce kicked off in June, starting with a business-to-consumer shopping mall; ranked #1 largest Taiwanese website by Business Next Publishing Corp.

2004

Partnered with Skype to develop the PCplanet & Skype VoIP service.

2005

Launched store department , offers SME e-commerce entry solution, which, besides B2C online shopping and C2C auction, offers a new business model to help SMEs in Taiwan get into e-commerce.

2006

PCplanet Online partners with eBay to create new online auction company.

2007

PCplanet 24h shopping launched. Received a positive response from the users and established new benchmarks in the Taiwanese e-commerce industry. Order forms immediately processed and products immediately delivered. 100% delivery rate achieved.

2008

Entered into the real estate industry by forming a joint venture with Century 21,Pacific Realtor, Chinatrust Real Estate Co., H&B Business Group, and Sinyi Realty Inc. to establish Rakuya.

2010

PCplanetSTORE INC. grew out of the store department of PCplanet Online Inc. in October 2005, and was spun-off as a PCplanet Online Inc. subsidiary in April 2010.
PCplanet global shopping began targeting the international market.

2011

PCplanet & Skype transferred operations to its subsidiary, LinkTel Inc.

2012

PCplanet US shopping website officially online, to better serve the Chinese community in North America.

2012

Established a third-party payment service “PCplanetPay”; in March of the following year, began offering a credit card payment collection service.
